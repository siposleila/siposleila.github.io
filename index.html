About me

I'm Leila Sipos, a 24 years old junior web-developer. I have been living in Visegrad for two years.

A few words about me:

Afrer I graduated from highschool I started to work in a pub near my village because I wanted to start the University and I had to pay for it myself. It was not a dream job, but I liked it. I enjoyed to work all day amongst people.

In 2015, I got accepted at the University, so I started to learn as a kindergarten teacher. It was not easy to study next to my job, but I passed all of my exams in the first year and almost all of them were exellent. Unfortunately I could not earn as much money as I needed, so I had to stop the course.

In 2017, I started to learn as a hospitality organizer, - I have a certificate about this course, - while I was still working in the same bar. In the summer of 2017 I got a really good job at Hotel Silvanus, a four stars hotel in Visegrad. As a receptionist I dealt with guests and reservations, I issued invoices and solved the complaints of the guests. This job taught me what is a real team-work. Selling hotel rooms and services were my favorites, and I was really good at it.

The web development began to interest me at the end of 2019. I found an online course which taught me the basic knowledge about HTML, CSS, JavaSript, JQery, and GitHub. It was love at first sight. Since then I have been striving to develop this knowledge. It is the beginning of a long road, but I do not mind because it contributes to my personal growth and my professional development.
